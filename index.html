About DriveGenie

DriveGenie offers a safe, customizable, and efficient simulation platform to test autonomous driving technologies. Built on CARLA, it allows diverse scenario testing with customizable sensor suites, dynamic weather, and integrated traffic scenarios. Our platform helps reduce costs and improve efficiency in the development of self-driving technologies.
